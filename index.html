Financial Status of the Average US American

There are many aspects that can cause seperation between groups of people. Specifically, the income gap is one of the most common things that cause seperation among groups of people in the US. Movements such as " I am the 99%" and "Occupy Wallstreet" have shed light on these dividing issues in the past. In this project I wanted to probe deeper into the separating factors that plague the US. The following charts provided by the Pew Research Center will provide information regarding the issues that people in the US face in statistical and graphical forms.

The first chart shown will focus on the personal finances of Americans on a national scale documented by the Pew Research Center.

Information for first graph: http://www.pewresearch.org/data-trend/national-conditions/personal-finances/
